5  Accommodation in Germany

  1. we are leaving the end of sequences as a “void” (Not NA) to be able to categorize end-point as “last known”

  2. we restructure the categorisation of accommodation to do not have “other” as one of the main category but rather operate with theory-based categories, new categories are following:

  • BLUE COLOR: government supported
  • RED COLOR: temporary/dependent: (volunteer + ngo + collective centers + hostels + family/friends + employer)
  • GREEN COLOR: self-sufficient (rented + own)
  • YELLOW COLOR: in Ukraine
  • VIOLET COLOR: in other country
